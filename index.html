Introduction

My name is Matiaullah Khatibi, and I am a 26-year-old web developer based in London. From a young age, I've had a deep fascination with computers and technology. The joy of exploring and tinkering with them sparked my interest in coding.

Initially, I dabbled in coding but felt uncertain about pursuing it further. However, as time went on, I realized that coding had a powerful creative and problem-solving potential that intrigued me. I delved into HTML and was pleasantly surprised by its versatility and the possibilities it offered.

During my college years, I studied IT and honed my coding skills. However, a detour into games design and animation during university made me realize that my true passion lies in coding. I made the decision to leave university and focus on my coding journey.

Since then, I've been on a dedicated path of self-learning and professional growth. Through coding challenges and projects, I've gained hands-on experience and a deeper understanding of web development. I am passionate about creating intuitive and user-friendly websites that combine aesthetics with functionality.

Now, I'm excited to embark on a rewarding career in the coding industry and continue expanding my knowledge and skills. Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out to me with any inquiries or opportunities. Let's bring ideas to life through the power of coding!
